Building damage assessment tutorial

Hi! This is a demo application that shows how you can perform a structural building damage analysis from in photos using the PyBossa framework in Crowdcrafting.org.

Volunteers browse the internet looking for photos relevant to a particular disaster, which are then loaded in this application. For each task one of these photos will be displayed to you and you will be asked a series of questions about the buildings or buildings that you see in the photos. Click on Next to go through a quick tutorial before starting the analysis.

The application will load a photo such as the one shown below and ask you the question: Is there a building or ruins in this photo?

You will have 3 possible answers:

  • Yes,
  • No, and
  • I don't know

All you have to do is to click in one of the three possible answers and, if you answer yes, you will move onto the next question. If you answer No or I don't know you will move onto the next photo.

Next you will be asked if this photo can be geo-located.

You will have 3 possible answers:

  • Yes,
  • No, and
  • I don't know

You can use the "Context" link to see where the photo came from and look for the names of any towns or other clues as to its location. Once you have answered you will move onto the next question

You will then be asked a series of questions about the damage that you see in the building that you are looking at

If you are not sure what a questions means, you can get help and explanations by clicking on the "Help" button for that question
